Welcome to Vidyaranya Trust ®

Vidyaranya Trust is a non-profit non-political and secular development organisation established on 23rd Sep 2015 in a village or place called Jakkasandra. Trust ambition is to remove illiteracy from the society and to ban child labour. The children who are below poverty line basically from remote areas who are unable to get daily basic needs like food, proper shelter, medical facility, and clothes, proper education, those people are there in Vidyaranya trust and like normal people even these children also leading there lives, giving proper education making them to analize there talent and improve there knowledge, Vidyaranya trust is working on these things the children who are orphan having single parents unable to educate those families can come forward and can leave their childern in our organisation and we are here to take care of them by giving a bright future to them.

If everybody would be willing to simply do something to care for one of these precious treasures (Little ones).

Our priority and vision is to see orphaned and left out children released from physical, economic & spiritual poverty, reach their highest and fullest potential.

The primary goal of the project is to restore hope and provision of basic needs to orphans and vulnerable children
